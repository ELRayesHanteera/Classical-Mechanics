# Chapter 1 — Survey of the Elementary Principles

Goldstein 3e, Chapter 1.

## Chapter goals

After completing this chapter you should be able to:

- State and apply Newton's laws in inertial and non-inertial frames
- Work with constraints and degrees of freedom
- Understand virtual displacements and virtual work
- Apply D'Alembert's principle to simple systems
- Connect mechanics to variational ideas (preview)
